# Phase 3f: Place Houses & Gardens

> House-Garden combo tokens and Garden tokens are **limited** — if none are available, you cannot perform the corresponding action.

Each **New Business Developer** you play allows you to either:

- **Place** a new house-garden combo token; **or**
- **Add** a garden token to an existing house on the map that does not already have a garden.

## Place a House-Garden Combo Token

Take a house-garden combo token, of any number, that is still available in the supply; place it anywhere on the map (**unlimited Range**) with the following rules:

- It must cover only **empty squares**.
- At least part of any of its edges must be **orthogonally adjacent to any road**.

You **cannot** choose to place a house without a garden.

> **Strategy Tip:** Placing house 14 this way allows you to prevent house 15 from having a garden (and they also can't benefit from a garden shared with house 15). Orienting the house so that the garden isn't adjacent to house 15 is helpful to remember this.

## Add a Garden

Take a garden from the supply and place it next to any house on the map (**unlimited Range**) that does not already have a garden with the following rules:

- It must cover only **empty squares**.
- Place it so that the house and garden together form a **2×3 rectangle**.

A garden can only belong to and influence a **single house**; so if a garden is placed adjacent to multiple houses, orient the garden so it is clear which house it belongs to.

## Houses with Gardens

A house with a garden can have up to **5 demand counters** on it (instead of 3); additionally, they pay **double the price** for each item they buy.
