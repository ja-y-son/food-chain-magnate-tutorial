# Phase 3b: Train Employees

> Train actions are **optional**; you may or may not resolve their effect.

Each Trainer, Coach, and Guru in your company structure provides 1 or more Train actions. For each Train action, perform the following steps:

1. **Select** 1 training option on an employee that is On the Beach (with limitations, see below).
2. **Return** the employee to the supply and take an employee of the newly-trained type.
3. **Place** the newly-trained employee On the Beach.

## Training an Employee Multiple Times

You **cannot** use multiple different employees to Train the same employee multiple times during the same turn (unless you have the "First to Pay $20 in Salaries" milestone).

However, you **can** use a single employee who provides multiple Train actions on the same employee.

> **Example 1:** 2 Trainers cannot Train your Errand Boy into a Truck Driver during the same turn.
>
> **Example 2:** You can use a Guru to Train an Errand Boy into a Zeppelin Pilot via 3 consecutive Train actions.

You can train employees you Hired this turn since they are On the Beach.

## Employee Card Limits

If none are available to take from the supply, you cannot Hire or Train that employee (until some are returned); however, if you train multiple steps, or hire and immediately train a person, the **intermediate cards do not have to be available**.

> **Example:** You have a Hire and Train action available to you. There are no Management Trainees in the supply to take, so you cannot Hire and take one alone, but you can combine your Hire and Train action to immediately take a New Business Developer.

> **Multiple Skip Example:** You've played a Recruiting Girl and a Guru this turn which provides 3 Train actions. There are no Management Trainees, Junior Vice Presidents, or Vice Presidents in the supply. You Hire a Management Trainee and immediately use the 3 Train actions to get a Regional Manager!

## Training Paths

All possible training paths are shown on your menu. Kitchen employees, drink collectors, and Marketeers each have their own training paths. All other higher-level employees start as Management Trainees and branch out from the management path.

### Unique Employees (1×)

Unique employees are highly trained individuals who fill special positions in your company. You can only have **1 maximum** of each unique employee type (including those On the Beach). You cannot Train an employee into a unique type you already have.
