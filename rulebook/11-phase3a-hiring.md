# Phase 3a: Hire Employees

> Hire actions are **optional**; you may or may not resolve their effect.

Your CEO provides **1 Hire action** each round. Each Recruiting Girl, Recruiting Manager, and HR Director that you played provide additional Hire actions.

For each Hire action, take any **1 entry-level employee** from the supply and place it On the Beach.

> **Expansion Only Rule:** When playing with the expansion, if you hire any employees who require a salary, they will need to be paid (or fired) later this round.

## Recruiter Salary Discounts

Actions provided by Recruiting Managers and HR Directors can be used to either **Hire employees** or receive **salary discounts** during the Payday phase. Hiring employees is optional, but salary discounts are mandatory; this means that if you do not use the full number of actions provided by a Recruiting Manager or HR Director in your company structure, you must use each of their leftover actions to get a **$5 salary discount** during Payday.
