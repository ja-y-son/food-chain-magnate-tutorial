# Phase 2: Order of Business

In this phase, players determine **turn order** as follows:

1. Remove all tokens from the turn order track and place them underneath it, maintaining the same order.
2. The player with the **greatest number of open Work slots** in their company structure chooses a turn-order position first, then the player with the second-most open slots chooses next, and so on, until all players have chosen.
   - When choosing, place your turn order token on any available position on the turn order track.

## First Airplane Campaign Milestone

Each player with this milestone counts an **extra 2 open Work slots** towards their total.

## Open Work Slot Ties

If 2 or more players have the same number of open slots, the tied player who was **previously ahead in turn order** chooses first. In the first round, all players will be tied, so players choose their position in the randomized starting order.

## Playing with Fewer Than 5 Players

Players can never choose any turn order position higher than the current player count.

> **Example:** In a 3-player game, a player cannot choose the 4th, 5th, or 6th position. If a player has already chosen spot "2", only spot "1" or "3" can be chosen by the next player.
