# Milestones Reference

## First to Train an Employee

- **Requirement:** Train an employee on your turn.
- **Benefit:** For the rest of the game, you must pay $15 less in salary during the Payday phase.

## First to Hire 3 Employees in 1 Turn

- **Requirement:** Hire 3 or more employees on your turn.
- **Benefit:** Immediately hire 2 additional Management Trainees. If there are not enough left in the supply, hire as many as you can. Cannot be combined with a Train to skip unavailable Management Trainees.

## First to Pay $20 or More in Salaries

- **Requirement:** Pay $20 or more in salaries in the Payday phase, after salary discounts.
- **Benefit:** For the rest of the game, you can use multiple Trainers, Coaches, and Gurus to train the same employee during the same turn. For example, you can use 2 Trainers and 1 Coach to train a Management Trainee 4 steps in 1 turn.

## First Waitress Played

- **Requirement:** Play a Waitress in the Restructuring phase.
- **Benefit:** At the end of each Dinnertime phase, earn a +$2 cash bonus for each Waitress played in your company structure. This includes the Waitress played to earn this milestone.

## First to Have $20

- **Requirement:** Have $20 or more in cash at the end of the Dinnertime phase.
- **Benefit:** Immediately look at all face-down reserve cards. You can look at them again at any time. You are allowed to make truthful or confusing noises about whatever you see on the cards, but cannot show them to other players.

## First to Have $100

- **Requirement:** Have $100 or more in cash at the end of the Dinnertime phase.
- **Benefit:** Beginning in the next Dinnertime phase, your CEO provides the same cash bonus as a CFO (50% of the total cash you earn during Dinnertime, rounded up). If you already have a CFO, you must fire them. You cannot train another CFO for the rest of the game.

## First Errand Boy Played

- **Requirement:** Play an Errand Boy in the Restructuring phase.
- **Benefit:** For the rest of the game, each of your Errand Boys collect 2 drinks of the same type, and each of your other drink collectors gain 1 extra drink from each supplier they visit (Cart Operators and Zeppelin Pilots collect 3 drinks from each supplier; Truck Drivers collect 4 drinks from each supplier).

## First Cart Operator Played

- **Requirement:** Play a Cart Operator in the Restructuring phase.
- **Benefit:** For the rest of the game, each of your Cart Operators, Truck Drivers, and Zeppelin Pilots have +1 Range. Errand Boys are not affected.

## First Burger / Pizza Produced

- **Requirement:** Produce at least 1 burger/pizza on your turn.
- **Benefit:** Immediately gain a Burger/Pizza Cook. You must pay their salary (or fire them) in the Payday phase. Because this milestone is earned after training employees on your turn, you cannot train your new Cook this round. If there are no Burger/Pizza Cooks left in the supply, you receive no benefit from this milestone.

## First to Throw Away Food or Drink

- **Requirement:** Throw away at least 1 unsold item during the Cleanup phase.
- **Benefit:** Immediately gain a freezer tile from the supply. Beginning in the next Cleanup phase, you can store up to 10 unsold items in your freezer at the end of the round instead of throwing them away. Items in your freezer are still in your stock and can be sold at Dinnertime. If you have more than 10 unsold items during Cleanup, you must choose 10 to store and throw away the rest. You can store the same items for multiple rounds.

## First to Lower Prices

- **Requirement:** Lower your item price during the Dinnertime phase.
- **Benefit:** For the rest of the game, your item price is $1 lower. This modifier applies during the current Dinnertime phase, and continues to apply every round, even when you do not play any pricing employees.

## First Burger / Pizza / Drink Marketed

- **Requirement:** Launch a campaign advertising burgers/pizza/any drink on your turn.
- **Benefit:** For the rest of the game, you earn a +$5 cash bonus for each burger/pizza/drink you sell. In the case of drinks, this bonus applies to all drinks you sell. This bonus does not affect your item price; it only affects the amount of cash you earn from each sale.

## First Billboard Campaign

- **Requirement:** Launch a billboard campaign on your turn.
- **Benefit:** For the rest of the game, you do not pay salaries for Campaign Managers, Brand Managers, or a Brand Director. This discount applies to both Marketeers in your company structure and Marketeers who are busy running campaigns. Additionally, for the rest of the game, all marketing campaigns you launch are **eternal**.

## First Airplane Campaign

- **Requirement:** Launch an airplane campaign on your turn.
- **Benefit:** In each Order of Business phase, count 2 additional open Work slots toward your total. You cannot play employees in these slots; they are only counted when choosing turn order.

## First Radio Campaign

- **Requirement:** Launch a radio campaign on your turn.
- **Benefit:** In each Marketing phase, each of your radio campaigns adds 2 demand counters to each house they reach (instead of one). This includes the radio campaign used to earn this milestone. If there is not enough space to place both demand counters on a house, add as many as you can. You still only remove 1 duration counter from a radio campaign each time it runs.
