# Phase 3c: Open Drive-Ins

If you played a **Local Manager** and/or a **Regional Manager**, you **must** place a "Drive-In" sign on each of your open restaurants. Each restaurant with a "Drive-In" sign is treated as having **entrances at all 4 corners** until its sign is removed during the Cleanup phase at the end of the round.

Unlike other actions during your turn, placing "Drive-In" signs is **mandatory**, meaning that if you have any Local or Regional Manager employees in your company structure, you must place signs on all your open restaurants.

> **NOTE:** Using a Local or Regional Manager to place or move a restaurant later during your turn is still optional.

## Drive-In Restaurants

Drive-in restaurants may create road connections to new locations on the map, or shorten the distance to previously-connected locations. Your employees who take actions on the map can start traveling from any Entrance Corner of a drive-in restaurant. The distance from a drive-in restaurant to a house is always counted from the Entrance Corner that is the shortest distance away from the house.

> **Example:** Without the drive-in, the restaurant is 1 distance away from house 15. With the drive-in, the restaurant is NOW zero distance away to house 15, because the closest Entrance Corner is now adjacent.
