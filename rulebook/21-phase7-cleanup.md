# Phase 7: Cleanup

After the Marketing phase, follow these steps to end the round:

1. **Throw Away Items:** All players must return any unsold items in their stock to the supply. (If a player has the "First to Throw Away Food or Drink" milestone, they can keep up to 10 items in their freezer instead of throwing them away.)
2. **Return Employees:** Each player returns all played employees in their company structure and On the Beach to their hand. Busy Marketeers are left face-up.
   > *This can be a good time to ask to see an opponent's cards.*
3. **Remove Signs:** Remove all "Coming Soon" and "Drive-In" signs on restaurants. All restaurants on the map are now open and all restaurants no longer have drive-ins.
4. **Cross Out Milestones:** Each player must place an ✗ token on each milestone they no longer are able to claim.
5. **New Round:** Begin the next round with the Restructuring phase. All duration counters on marketing campaigns and all demand counters on houses remain in play.

## Cleanup Milestones

If you throw away at least 1 food or drink item during the Cleanup phase while the "First to Throw Away Food or Drink" milestone is available, immediately claim the milestone. Although you gain a new freezer immediately, you may **not** use it to store unsold items until the following Cleanup phase, so you must still throw away all unsold items you currently have.

## Game End

After the bank breaks for the second time, finish the Dinnertime phase, then end the game. **Do not** resolve the remaining phases of the round.

> **Note:** You do not need to pay salaries in the last round — no need to fire your whole staff!

Each player counts up their total cash (including any "IOUs" they have written down). Whoever has the **most cash wins**. In the event of a tie, the tied player who is ahead in turn order wins.
