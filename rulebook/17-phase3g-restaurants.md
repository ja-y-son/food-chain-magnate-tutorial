# Phase 3g: Place or Move Restaurants

> **NOTE:** Remember that both Local and Regional Managers also add drive-ins to all your open restaurants during the Open Drive-Ins step. Restaurants are **limited**: if all 3 of your restaurants are already on the map, you cannot place a new one. You can still move a restaurant using a Regional Manager.

## Using a Local Manager

Each Local Manager you play allows you to place a new restaurant with its **"Coming Soon"** side face up (that will open at the end of the round) as follows:

Place it within a **road Range of 3** as follows:

1. Start on an Entrance Corner of one of your open restaurants.
2. Continue to an orthogonally-adjacent section of road.
3. Trace a line of distance 3, along connected roads, to the desired location for the new restaurant. The number 3 tells you the maximum number of times your traced road route can cross a tile border.

The restaurant must occupy only **empty squares** and its entrance must connect to any road (the new restaurant must still connect to the road the Local Manager used for their path).

> **Example:** The new restaurant is legally placed as it is connected (via its entrance) to any road on the map tile that the Local Manager can get to with 3 Range from one of your existing restaurants. The "Coming Soon" side of the token must be showing face up on the new restaurant as it is not yet open.

## Using a Regional Manager

A Regional Manager you play allows you to either: **place a new open restaurant**; or, **relocate one of your restaurants**.

### Place an Open Restaurant

Place a new open restaurant **anywhere on the map** (unlimited Range); it must occupy only empty squares and its entrance must connect to a road. Place a "Drive-In" sign on top of it.

The restaurant is immediately open with its "open" side placed face up, and additionally has a drive-in.

### Relocate a Restaurant

Alternatively, you can remove one of your open restaurants from the map, then place it **anywhere on the map** (it stays open), keeping its Drive-In sign (unlimited Range); it must occupy only empty squares and its entrance must connect to a road.

> **NOTE:** You may rotate a restaurant and return it to the same spot provided the entrance stays adjacent to a road.
