# Strategic Pointers

The best strategy in the game is highly dependent on the map layout, your map position, and the actions of other players (in no particular order).

- **Milestones are key.** Milestones are the key to planning your strategy. You will need to claim milestones before others do. Reaching the right milestones as soon as possible is often essential, as the others will likely be taken by the time you reach them.

- **Hiring vs. Earning.** There is a trade-off between building up your company through hiring and training, and earning cash through marketing and sales. The winning mix depends on how long the game ends up taking. This in turn depends on the bank reserve cards players chose, and whether more players choose to focus on recruiting and training for the long game or making early sales to break the bank quickly.

- **Late-game acceleration.** The game often accelerates dramatically towards the end. You can win by earning cash only in the last round, if you have prepared well — or you could still be preparing for a round that never comes.

- **Strategic advertising.** You can hurt other players by advertising items they cannot produce. But beware, you may also be helping them sell more later on!

- **House order matters.** Try to plan around the order that houses will go out for dinner, because if you serve all of your stock to lower-numbered houses, you will not be able to serve higher-numbered houses that would otherwise buy from you. In some cases, this can lead to customers who go out later in the evening traveling a very long way (or paying a very steep price) to get what they want.

- **Price wars.** Price wars are very effective to attack others, but they will be very costly to yourself as well. Use them tactically.

- **Gardens.** Houses with gardens are very lucrative but may attract competition.

- **Quality vs. quantity.** You can sometimes make more cash selling a few items at high prices than by selling a lot of items at a discount. But it can also be the other way around.

- **Cooperative advertising.** Sometimes, it can be useful to advertise in a way that helps other players earn cash — as long as you earn more yourself!

- **Combine strategies.** Combine the previous ideas for maximum effect.
