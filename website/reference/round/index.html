FCM Tutorial

Round Summary

Every round follows the same 7 phases in strict order. Phases 1–3 require decisions; 4–7 are automatic.

1 Phase 1: Restructuring Decision

Everyone simultaneously decides which employees to play and which to bench.

  • Place employees to work face-down below your CEO.
  • Place employees to bench face-down above (On the Beach).
  • Everyone reveals at the same time.

⚠️

If you assign more employees than you have slots, ALL go On the Beach!

2 Phase 2: Order of Business Decision

Determine turn order based on empty work slots. More empty slots = pick your position first.

  • Ties broken by previous turn order.
  • Going first is often very valuable for hiring.
3 Phase 3: Working 9-5 Decision

In turn order, each player resolves sub-phases:

  1. Hire — CEO gives 1 free hire; Recruiters give more.
  2. Train — Upgrade employees that are On the Beach (requires Trainer).
  3. Drive-Ins — Mandatory with Local/Regional Manager.
  4. Marketing — Place campaign tokens (Marketeers become busy).
  5. Produce food & collect drinks — Kitchen staff produce; Errand Boys/Carts/Trucks collect.
  6. Place houses & gardens — New Business Developer action.
  7. Place/move restaurants — Local/Regional Manager action.
4 Phase 4: Dinnertime 💰 Auto

No decisions. Houses checked in numerical order:

  1. Does it have demand? → If no, skip.
  2. Is it connected by road to a restaurant? → If no, skip.
  3. Does the restaurant have ALL demanded items? → If no, skip.
  4. One restaurant qualifies → Sale! ($10/item, $20/item with garden)
  5. Multiple qualify → Competition: lowest effective price wins.

Demand counters removed after sale. Game ends if bank can't pay.

5 Phase 5: Payday Skipped

SKIPPED in the Introductory Game. No salaries to pay. In the full game, you pay each employee's salary from your cash.

6 Phase 6: Marketing Auto

Active campaigns run in numerical order:

  • Place 1 demand counter on each house within reach.
  • Remove 1 duration counter from the campaign.
  • Campaign ends when duration hits 0 → Marketeer freed.

Houses max out at 3 demand (5 with garden).

7 Phase 7: Cleanup Auto
  • Discard all unsold food/drinks (no carryover!).
  • Return employees to hand (busy Marketeers stay out).
  • Remove Drive-In and Coming Soon signs.
  • Start the next round!

⚠️ Key Timing

Marketing (Phase 6) happens AFTER Dinnertime (Phase 4). Campaigns launched this round create demand that won't result in sales until next round!
