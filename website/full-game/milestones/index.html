FCM Tutorial

Milestones

Permanent bonuses you earn by being first to achieve something.

What Are Milestones?

Milestones are one-time achievements that grant permanent abilities for the rest of the game. The first player to meet a milestone's condition claims it — no one else can earn that same milestone. They are the biggest difference between the introductory and full game.

📌 Key Rule

Milestones are checked at specific moments. When you meet the condition, you immediately claim the card. Planning around milestones is central to full-game strategy.

Hiring & Training Milestones

MilestoneConditionBenefit
First to Train Train an employee Pay $15 less in salary every round (permanent discount)
First to Hire 3 in 1 Turn Hire 3+ employees in a single turn Immediately gain 2 free Management Trainees
First to Pay $20+ in Salaries Pay $20+ in salaries (after discounts) Use multiple trainers on the same employee in one turn (e.g., 2 Trainers + 1 Coach = 4 training steps)

Strategy: Which Milestones to Chase?

💡 Tip

You can't get every milestone. Pick 2-3 that fit your strategy and race for those. The marketed-item milestones (+$5 per sale) are incredibly powerful if you sell in volume. "First to Train" gives the best salary relief.

⚠️ Watch Out

Some milestones have hidden costs. "First Burger/Pizza Produced" gives you an employee you must pay salary for. "First to Have $100" forces you to fire your CFO. Always weigh the ongoing cost against the benefit.
