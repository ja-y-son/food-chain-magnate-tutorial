Phase 7: Cleanup & Next Round

Phase 7 is quick but critical — it resets the board for the next round. Follow these steps in order:

1. Discard Unsold Stock

ALL unsold food and drinks are discarded back to the supply. Nothing carries over to the next round — your stock starts empty every time.

⚠️ Watch Out

You lose ALL unsold food at end of round. There is no carryover. Don't overproduce — only make what you can actually sell this round! Every wasted item is a wasted action.

2. Return Employees

All employees return from your work structure back to your hand. The exception: busy Marketeers remain locked in your structure until their campaign ends.

3. Remove Signs

Drive-In signs are removed — your restaurants revert to having only their original entrance corner. Coming Soon signs are also removed — those restaurants are now open for business starting next round.

4. Start Next Round

Go back to Phase 1 (Restructuring) and begin the next round. Decide which employees to play, which to bench, and keep building your fast food empire.

Game End

The game ends when the bank can't fully pay for a sale during Dinnertime. Finish resolving the current Dinnertime phase (all remaining houses are checked), then count cash. The player with the most cash wins.

📌 Key Rule

In the introductory game, the bank breaks once and the game ends. In the full game, the bank breaks twice — but don't worry about that for now.

💡 Tip

The bank is the clock. Every dollar earned by any player drains the bank. If everyone is selling a lot, the game ends fast. Watch the bank — when it's getting low, make sure you've already converted your engine into cash.
