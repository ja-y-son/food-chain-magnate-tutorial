Phase 6: Marketing

Campaigns Run Automatically

Just like Dinnertime, marketing campaigns resolve automatically. Active campaigns are processed in numerical order based on their position on the board.

Campaign Reach

Each campaign type reaches houses differently. Select a tab to see how each one works:

Billboard

Reaches houses on orthogonally adjacent tiles (up, down, left, right — not diagonal). The simplest campaign type with the smallest reach.

Mailbox

Reaches houses in a line-of-sight in all 4 orthogonal directions from the campaign tile. The line extends until it hits a road or the map edge.

⚠️ Watch Out

Roads block mailbox reach. This is unique to mailboxes — no other campaign type is blocked by roads. Two roads touching at a corner also block the line.

Airplane

Reaches houses in a flyover zone — a broad area 1, 3, or 5 rows wide depending on the Marketeer level. The airplane flies in a straight line across the entire board.

Radio

Reaches houses on the same tile and all 8 surrounding tiles (including diagonals) — a full 3×3 area. The most powerful campaign type with the widest reach.

How Campaigns Run

During the Marketing phase (Phase 6), campaigns resolve automatically in numerical order (by campaign token number):

  1. Each active campaign places 1 demand counter of its advertised type on every house within its reach.
  2. Remove 1 duration counter from the campaign.
  3. If no duration counters remain, the campaign ends and the Marketeer goes On the Beach (freed for next round).

💡 Tip

Campaign number matters strategically. Lower-numbered campaigns resolve first and are more likely to successfully place demand (since houses have a demand cap). Getting a low-numbered campaign token is a real advantage.

Demand Cap

Each house can hold a maximum of 3 demand counters. If a house has a garden, the cap increases to 5.

Campaigns that would push a house over its cap simply have no effect on that house. You cannot remove existing demand to make room for yours.

Campaign Duration

Each round, 1 duration counter is removed from each active campaign. When the last counter is removed, the campaign ends and the Marketeer who placed it returns to your hand.

⚠️ Watch Out

Houses cap at 3 demand counters (5 with garden). Additional marketing on a maxed-out house is wasted — plan your campaigns to target houses that still have room.
