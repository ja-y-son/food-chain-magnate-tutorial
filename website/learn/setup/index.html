Map Setup

  1. Lay out random map tiles in the correct grid size, randomly oriented.
  2. Verify all 3 drink suppliers (soda, lemonade, beer) are on the map — swap tiles if needed.
  3. Place the house & garden tokens, campaign tokens, and food/drink tokens near the map.
  4. Fill the bank with $75 per player.
  5. Set up the employee supply tray.

Placing Your First Restaurant

In reverse turn order, each player places their first restaurant on the map (or passes):

  • Must cover exactly 4 empty squares — no roads, no printed locations.
  • The entrance corner (marked on the token) must be adjacent to at least 1 road.
  • No two starting restaurant entrances can be on the same map tile.

⚠️ Watch Out

Your entrance corner determines everything — customer access, drink collection routes, marketing reach, and competitive distance. A poorly placed entrance can cut you off from the best neighborhoods. Look at where drink suppliers are and where houses could be placed later!
